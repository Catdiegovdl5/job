Performance Creative Strategy & Data-Driven Ads | RYZE

This project was developed as a strategic creative test for a high-growth e-commerce brand, focusing on Meta and Google Ads performance. The primary objective was to engineer assets that optimize CTR and lower CPA through data-backed design principles.

Strategy & Execution

  • ▪ Native Ads (UGC Style): Created to bypass 'ad blindness' by mimicking organic content, specifically designed for the critical 2-second 'stop-the-scroll' window.
  • ▪ Static Performance Ads: Developed high-intent layouts utilizing split-screen 'Struggle vs. Answer' visual hierarchy to trigger immediate consumer decision-making.
  • ▪ Psychological Triggers: Benefit-driven messaging focused on solving core pain points (Jitters, Brain Fog, Afternoon Crash) with proven functional solutions.

Technical Workflow

  • ▪ AI-Prototyping: Utilized advanced AI tools for rapid scenario generation and realistic product integration.
  • ▪ Infrastructure: Powered by an i5 13th Gen / 16GB RAM hardware setup for zero-lag editing and high-fidelity output (Optimized for thermal safety).
  • ▪ SOP Adherence: Strictly followed creative briefs and Standard Operating Procedures (SOP) to ensure technical alignment for high-spend advertising accounts.

Tracking & Attribution

  • ▪ Technical Stack: Implementation of GTM (Google Tag Manager) and Meta Conversions API (CAPI) to ensure 100% data accuracy and bypass iOS14+ tracking limitations.

Engineering vs. Design

  • ▪ Iterative Testing: Assets are not just created; they are engineered based on psychological hooks. We run 'Dark Posts' to validate hooks before scaling the primary budget.
